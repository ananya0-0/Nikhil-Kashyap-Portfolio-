I've been working on Maya - an AI companion designed to remember you, understand you over time, and be honest with you instead of just agreeable.

Most AI assistants today are designed to please. They tell you what you want to hear. Maya is different, it's built to remember your history, understand your patterns, and give you honest feedback even when it's uncomfortable.

Maya comes with Halo, a hardware companion that gives it a real presence on your desk. Because an AI companion shouldn't just live in the cloud, it should have a physical anchor in your space.

This is the vision I'm pursuing at AlterAI.
